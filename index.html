About

Hello! I'm Nandan, currently in 2A Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I gained valuable industry experience during my first co-op, serving dual roles as an Automation Engineer with Quasar Consulting Group and Mechanical Engineering Intern with their subsidiary, Pratus Group.

I'm passionate about embedded systems and robotics, with a focus on firmware development and automation technologies. Currently, I work as both a Firmware and Hardware Engineer on Waterloo's Midnight Sun Solar Race Car Team. My goal is to contribute to the automotive industry through robotics automation and computer vision systems, working on projects that integrate hardware and software solutions for autonomous driving and intelligent manufacturing.

I am currently seeking opportunities to apply my skills in innovative engineering environments and contribute to cutting-edge technology development.
